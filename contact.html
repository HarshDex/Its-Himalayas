Quick Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 4–8 weeks in advance, especially for peak season (May–June). Batches fill up fast.

What's the booking process?

Send us an enquiry → we'll share a detailed proposal → 25% advance to confirm → balance 15 days before departure.

Do you offer instalment payments?

Yes — for bookings made 60+ days in advance, we offer a 3-part payment plan. Ask us about it.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, bank transfer, credit/debit cards, and cash. We provide a formal receipt for all payments.
